HOW TO PLAY MATCH THREE

Objective and Rules

Swap tiles to make matches of three (or more) of a kind. These matches may be horizontal or vertical. Each match and resulting cascade of matches (if you're lucky) scores you points! You have 30 moves to score as many points as possible. You may only move a tile if that move ensures at least a three-tile match.

CONTROLS

Use your cursor to select your desired tile. It will become outlined in Orange.
Once you have selected your tile, click on the tile with which you want to swap.
Press New Game to play again, or reset the game.
